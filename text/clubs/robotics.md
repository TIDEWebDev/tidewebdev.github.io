### TIDE Astrobots: Robotics Club

TIDE's robotics team, advised by [Bill McFarland](bill@billmcfarland.org) and
Camala Umbenhaur, meets on Mondays and Wednesdays after school in room 106.
Meetings usually go until 5.

Club president: [David Bustamante](mailto:822798@seq.org)

The club focuses on participating in tournaments and competitions for
[FIRST's FTC](https://www.firstinspires.org/programs/ftc/). TIDE Astrobots are
sponsored by the
[County Consumer Plumbing Group](https://www.plumbingservice.com/).

Robotics has many different roles. There are teams for robot construction,
programming, robot design, and publicity. If you are interested in any of these,
consider attending a meeting.
