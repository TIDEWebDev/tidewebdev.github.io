TIDE Astrobots: Robotics Club

TIDE's robotics team, advised by Bill McFarland and Camala Umbenhaur, meets on Mondays and Wednesdays after school in room 106. Meetings usually go until 5.

Club president: David Bustamante

The club focuses on participating in tournaments and competitions for FIRST's FTC. TIDE Astrobots are sponsored by the County Consumer Plumbing Group.

Robotics has many different roles. There are teams for robot construction, programming, robot design, and publicity. If you are interested in any of these, consider attending a meeting.
